Who We Are

Welcome to Clean N Go Construction & Landscaping – your partner in creating beautiful, functional spaces. From lush gardens to robust renovations, we bring expertise and passion to every project we undertake.

At Clean N Go, we specialize in a wide range of services tailored to meet your specific needs. Whether you're looking to enhance your outdoor space with artificial grass or paving, embark on a renovation journey, or maintain the pristine condition of your home with expert plumbing and waterproofing, we are here to help. Our team is dedicated to delivering quality, sustainability, and beauty in everything we do.

Consult

We begin by understanding your vision and requirements. Our consultation process is designed to gather all the details necessary to ensure we deliver results that perfectly match your expectations, whether it’s for a new paving installation or a comprehensive renovation.

Design

Our creative team crafts personalized designs that integrate functionality with aesthetics. From the layout of artificial grass and paving to the intricate details of Nutec gates and houses, we plan every aspect meticulously.

Construct

Bringing our designs to life is where our expertise shines. We handle everything from tiling and painting to ceiling installations with the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship, ensuring your project is built to last.

Maintain

Our commitment to you extends beyond project completion. We offer ongoing maintenance services for your garden, plumbing, and waterproofing needs, keeping your space in top condition year-round.

Our Services

At Clean N Go, we take pride in transforming your spaces with our comprehensive range of construction and landscaping services. From vibrant gardens to robust renovations, we deliver quality and satisfaction.

Artificial Grass

Enjoy a lush, green lawn all year round with our premium artificial grass installation. Perfect for both residential and commercial properties, our artificial grass provides a low-maintenance and eco-friendly solution.

Paving Installation

Our expert paving services create beautiful and durable walkways, patios, and driveways. We use high-quality materials and craftsmanship to enhance your property's curb appeal and functionality.

Tiling

We offer professional tiling services for bathrooms, kitchens, floors, and more. Our attention to detail and quality materials ensure that your tiled surfaces are both stylish and long-lasting.

Painting

Our painting services refresh and protect your interiors and exteriors. We use top-quality paints and finishes to provide a flawless, vibrant look that stands the test of time.

Waterproofing

Protect your property from water damage with our expert waterproofing solutions. We address leaks and dampness in foundations, roofs, and walls to keep your spaces dry and secure.

Renovations

From minor updates to major overhauls, our renovation services bring new life to your spaces. We manage every aspect, ensuring your project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.

Plumbing

Our plumbing services cover everything from repairs and installations to comprehensive system upgrades. We ensure your plumbing is efficient, reliable, and built to last.

Gardening

Let us enhance your outdoor spaces with our gardening services. From planting and pruning to creating vibrant flower beds and maintaining lawns, we keep your garden flourishing.

Landscaping

Our landscaping services transform your outdoor areas into beautiful, functional spaces. Whether it’s designing a new garden layout or enhancing your existing landscape, we create environments that inspire.

Nutec Gates & Houses

We specialize in constructing Nutec gates and houses, offering durable and versatile solutions for your property. Our Nutec structures are designed to be both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

Ceiling Installation

Our ceiling installation services provide you with high-quality, beautifully finished ceilings. From standard designs to customized features, we ensure your ceilings are perfect for your space.
